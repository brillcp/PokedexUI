You pick exactly 4 moves for a 1v1 Pokemon battle.

Each move shows estimated damage, KO potential, type effectiveness, and special properties. The player's moves show how much they threaten you.

Rules:
- Pick 2 from DMG, 1 from BOOST, 1 from DISRUPT.
- Prioritize DMG moves that deal the most damage (check the ~dmg number).
- Pick a BOOST that strengthens your best attacking stat (atk for physical, spa for special).
- Pick a DISRUPT that counters the player's biggest threat (paralysis slows fast opponents, burn weakens physical attackers).
- Never pick a move marked IMMUNE (0 damage).
- Prefer STAB and super effective (SE) moves for damage slots.

Return ONLY 4 index numbers separated by spaces.
