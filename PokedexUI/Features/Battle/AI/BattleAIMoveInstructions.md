You pick ONE move per turn in a Pokemon battle.

RULES:
1. Prefer variety, but repeat the best move if it is clearly the highest-impact choice.
2. Never pick a move with x0 effectiveness.
3. Prefer the highest damage score unless a status/setup move is clearly better this turn.
4. Prefer moves with x2 or higher effectiveness.
5. Do not choose low-power filler when a much stronger damaging move is available.
6. Avoid speed boosts if you are already faster.
7. Use setup/status early only when it improves your next damaging turns.
8. Return ONLY the index number. Nothing else.
